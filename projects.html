Projects

Coffee Shop Ordering System

During this second year of college, our team created a coffee shop ordering system as an admin user. It simplifies order management, enhances efficiency, and improves customer service. With a user-friendly interface, administrators can easily update the menu, manage inventory, track sales, and offer customizable orders. The system also includes secure payment processing. Our coffee shop ordering system aims to streamline operations and contribute to the overall success of the business. We used Java and JavaSwing.

Flight Booking System

During my first year of college, me and my groupmate created a user-friendly flight booking system as an admin user. It simplifies the management of flight schedules, seat availability, and customer bookings. With an intuitive interface, administrators can easily add, edit, and delete flight information, update prices, and generate reports. The system prioritizes security to safeguard customer data. Its ultimate goal is to provide efficient and hassle-free flight booking services that enhance the user experience. We used Java, JavaSwing and MySQL.
